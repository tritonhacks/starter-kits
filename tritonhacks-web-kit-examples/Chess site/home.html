The Growing Popularity of Chess

What is it

Chess is a recreational board game played by two players. The object of the game is to checkmate the opponent's king whereby the king is under "check" and has no further moves.

Demography

Chess is played by about 605 million adults today. It is played through a varied national demographic profile. Chess players tend to be significantly better than other players of games or sports in attributes such as intelligence and strategy and generally live in households with over $120,000 in income.

Popularity

Chess has recently enjoyed a large uptick of online interest for a variety of reasons though some of the biggest factors include the corona crisis, series of collaboration streams by prominent chess players (notable Hikaru Nakamura) with popular twitch streamers, and the release of the popular T.V show, The Queen's Gambit. As of 2021, chess has broken all-time records in numbers of views on sites such as Youtube and concurrent viewers on streaming sites such as Twitch. As chess becomes more popular though, the "old-guard" has began a gatekeeping backlash leading to questions on if chess' rising popularity is truly a good thing.
